About Me

My journey from design to development has shaped my unique approach to creating digital experiences that are both beautiful and functional.

2015 - The Beginning

Graduated with a degree in Graphic Design and started working as a junior designer at a creative agency in San Francisco.

"I was always fascinated by the intersection of aesthetics and functionality. My design background gave me a strong foundation in visual communication."

2017 - The Transition

Discovered my passion for web development and began learning HTML, CSS, and JavaScript while continuing design work.

"The ability to bring my designs to life through code was transformative. I spent countless nights learning to code, and it opened up a whole new world of possibilities."

2019 - Python Discovery

Fell in love with Python and began developing backend solutions, combining my frontend skills to create full-stack applications.

"Python's elegance and readability resonated with me. I found that my design thinking helped me write cleaner, more intuitive code."

2021 - Freelance Journey

Took the leap to become a freelance creative developer, working with clients across various industries on both design and development projects.

"Going independent allowed me to fully embrace my multidisciplinary approach. I could now offer clients end-to-end solutions from concept to deployment."

2023 - Present

Currently working as a creative technologist, specializing in crafting unique digital experiences that blend artistry with technical excellence.

"Today, I thrive in the space between design and development. My goal is to create work that not only looks beautiful but also solves real problems through thoughtful implementation."
